This is a joint field excursion with the Bucks Geology Group. The excursion will be held over two days and members can choose to attend one or both days.

### Programme

**Friday Aug 14th: Doulting Quarry (10:00am-4:00pm)**
The first day will be led by the Somerset Earth Science Centre (SEC) to Doulting Quarry, a working quarry where access is only available on a weekday. If time permits, we will be visiting a second working quarry as well (tbd).

The visit to the quarry will be in groups of 10 to 12 which is limited by the capacity of the centre’s minibuses. Strong boots and suitable waterproofs are a pre-requisite for the quarry tour. PPE is provided by the SEC. **Please note that the SEC invite donations of £15 for each person attending the tour.**

**Saturday Aug 15th: Vallis Vale (10:00am-2:00pm)**
The second day will be led by Graham Hickman and will visit two sites in Vallis Vale: the famous De La Beche Unconformity and the Tetbury Camp Quarry. We will examine close up the unconformity between the Carboniferous limestone and the Inferior Oolite, representing a time gap of around 170 million years. 

Bring strong boots and suitable waterproofs, but PPE will not be required for this day.
